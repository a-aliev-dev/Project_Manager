What is QuestBoard?

QuestBoard is an innovative project management system that transforms boring work routines into an exciting adventure. We believe that completing tasks should be as engaging as playing an RPG.

How It Works

⚔️ Tasks as Quests

Every work assignment is a quest. By completing it, you don't just check a box — you earn Experience Points (XP) and valuable trophies.

📈 Levels & Skills

Level up your character! Completing coding tasks boosts your "Code Magic" level, while closing documentation increases your "Intellect" stats.

🏆 Leaderboard & Competition

Track your colleagues' progress in real-time. Become a corporate legend by reaching the top of the weekly Global Heroes leaderboard.

Why It's Effective

  • Motivation: Game mechanics encourage employees to close tasks faster.
  • Engagement: Competitive elements bring the team together.
  • Transparency: All progress is visualized through charts and achievements.
